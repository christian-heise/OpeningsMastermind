## 3. Information about the data processing

Your data processed when using our app will be deleted or blocked as soon as the purpose for its storage ceases to apply, provided the deletion of the same is not in breach of any statutory storage obligations or unless otherwise stipulated below.

### 3.1 App data stored on your device

Your opening studies, game trees, practice history, and app settings are stored only locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to us and we have no access to it. It remains on your device until you delete the respective study or the app itself.

### 3.2 Analytics (TelemetryDeck)

To understand how the app is used and to improve it, the app uses the privacy-focused analytics service TelemetryDeck, operated by TelemetryDeck GmbH, Augsburg, Germany.

**Analytics is disabled by default.** No analytics or crash data (see section 3.3) leaves your device unless you actively enable it in Settings → Privacy → "Share Analytics". You can disable it again at any time.

While analytics is enabled, TelemetryDeck processes on our behalf:

- an anonymized, non-traceable user identifier generated per app installation (no advertising identifier is used);
- the events you trigger in the app (e.g. that a study was imported), defined by us;
- a timestamp rounded to the hour;
- device and app metadata: device model and brand, operating system and version, processor architecture, screen orientation and resolution, time zone, app version and build number, and your language, region and appearance (light/dark) preferences.

TelemetryDeck expressly does **not** collect IP addresses (neither in logs nor in the database), does not use cookies or comparable tracking technologies, and stores no identifiers that could be traced back to an individual person. Data is processed on servers in the European Union.

Legal basis for this processing is your consent pursuant to Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR, which you grant by enabling analytics and which you can withdraw at any time with effect for the future by disabling it in the app.

Further information: TelemetryDeck's privacy policy at https://telemetrydeck.com/privacy/

### 3.3 Crash reports

While analytics is enabled, the app also forwards diagnostic data about app crashes — collected by Apple's system framework MetricKit — to TelemetryDeck. This diagnostic data consists of technical crash information (such as anonymized stack traces) and contains no personal data. It is governed by the same setting as analytics (section 3.2): disabling analytics also disables crash reporting. Legal basis is your consent pursuant to Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (the same setting as in section 3.2).

### 3.4 Lichess

If you sign in with your Lichess account, the app uses Lichess's OAuth login procedure. An access token issued by Lichess is stored securely in your device's keychain; it is not transmitted to us. When you use the opening explorer or retrieve Lichess account information, the corresponding requests are sent to Lichess (lichess.org, explorer.lichess.ovh), operated by Lichess (Lichess is a non-profit organisation). Data processing by Lichess is governed by its own privacy policy at https://lichess.org/privacy. Legal basis for establishing this connection at your request is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (or Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR where you have consented by signing in).
